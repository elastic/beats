[[deprecations-8.19.17]]
=== Deprecations

The following functionality is deprecated in 8.19.17, and will be removed in
8.19.17. Deprecated functionality does not have an immediate impact on your
application, but we strongly recommend you make the necessary updates after you
upgrade to 8.19.17.




**Beats**

* Deprecate ssl.restart_on_cert_change in favor of ssl.certificate_reload. https://github.com/elastic/beats/pull/51318[#51318] https://github.com/elastic/beats/pull/51364[#51364] https://github.com/elastic/beats/pull/51365[#51365] https://github.com/elastic/beats/pull/51366[#51366] https://github.com/elastic/beats/pull/51367[#51367] { $k | section}}::$k }}::
